Chimichurri Sauce

-- anonimo
Porciones: 4

Ingredientes

  • 4.0 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 16.0 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 8.0 garlic clove, minced
  • 4.0 teaspoon sea salt
  • 4.0 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2.0 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2.0 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 4.0 cup finely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, (from about 1 bunch of parsley, coarse stems removed)

Instrucciones

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, garlic, salt, oregano, red pepper flakes, and smoked paprika. Stir in the parsley. Alternatively, you could combine the ingredients in a mortar and pestle or with a few pulses in a food processor.
  2. Season to taste and serve as a sauce over roasted or grilled vegetables.

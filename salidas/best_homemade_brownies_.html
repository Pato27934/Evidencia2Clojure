Best Homemade Brownies

-- Weeknight Baking by Michelle Lopez.
Porciones (6)

Ingredientes

  • 0.5625 cups 1/2 cups granulated sugar*
  • 0.28125 cup all-purpose flour
  • 0.25 cup cocoa powder, sifted
  • 0.1875 cup powdered sugar, sifted
  • 0.1875 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 0.28125 teaspoons sea salt
  • 0.75 large eggs
  • 0.1875 cup canola oil
  • 0.75 tablespoons water
  • 0.1875 teaspoon vanilla
Calorías totales: 869 kcal

Instrucciones

  1. Preheat the oven to 162.778°C.
  2. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the parchment paper.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla.
  5. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and use a spatula to smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached (note: it's better to pull the brownies out early than to leave them in too long).
  8. Cool completely before slicing.
